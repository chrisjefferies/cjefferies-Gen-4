I was tasked with a revitalization of the SNWorks corporate identity, website and interface design for their Suite of content management system software.

The starting point

Here's an example of the SNWorks website and identity as it was when I got there. One of the first projects I was assigned was submitting designs for a complete restart.

Making a statement

I focused on a clean, modern presentation with an edgy and bold feel appropriate for an organization staffed primarily by college students. In keeping some of the original design elements, I was able to preserve some of the brand equity. Once this hexagonal theme was established, many tertiary elements virtually created themselves.
