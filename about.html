I am a Graphic Designer from the greater Detroit area. I hold two associates degrees from Oakland Community College, in Graphic Design and Liberal Arts and a Bachelors of Fine Arts from Michigan State University.

I have completed many large scale design projects in a variety of industries, including print publication, engineering and manufactureing and web services. I am consistently striving to acquire a knowledge base from multiple disciplines, not only as a source of inspiration, but also to better inform designs targeted to a specific audience.

Having grown up with an engineer and former ameture drag racer for a father, I am also well versed in automotive tinkering. I have built several automotive systems including engines, breaks and exhaust. I am self taught MIG/TIG and Oxy-Asetelyne welder, and excell at basic and intermediate metal fabrication.

In addition, I am also interested in illustration, and am currently authoring an ongoing comic book called Trident 120, a military action thriller.
