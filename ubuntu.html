Date Produced:

Apr 2014

This short commercial, set to Deadmau5' instrumental Ghosts N Stuff, features kinetic typography and an overview of some of the work I've done with the modern, workhorse typeface. Ultimately, the feel of the video was crafted to reflect the feel of the type, and features verbiage that Canonical LTD used to describe it.

Assets were created in Adobe Illustrator and After Effects, and the audio was recut to fit the limited duration in Sony Sound Forge.
