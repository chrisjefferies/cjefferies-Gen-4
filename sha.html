Shadow League is an app design that connects pool sharks.

Through a theoretically possible series of sensors and devices, users could track games and leagues on their phones, picking up a broad array of statistics along the way.

Such an app needs to be light and flexible for the highest cross-platform compatibility. It also needs to be intuitive to use, with a tertiary focus on collecting and organizing a surprising amount of data.
