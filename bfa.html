I was happily a member of Michigan State's Bachelor of Fine Art class of 2014. With a handful of other designers, I helped craft the marketing and exhibition materials.

In order to execute this animation, I started with the event ephemera designed by Erica Tedesco, including poster (below), post card and business card designs.

My highest priority was using the existing design language to pack as much impact as I could into a less than 15 second clip. I also focused on using the motion design to reflect the look and feel of the original design elements.

Designed by Erica Tedesco
